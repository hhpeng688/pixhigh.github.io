Dracula: Bloodlines of Desire

A Gothic Romance Visual Novel set in Neo-Victorian Europe, 2125. Choose your path between humanity and bloodlust in this fully voiced interactive story.

About the Game

Dracula: Bloodlines of Desire is a choice-driven visual novel combining a dual morality system, fully-voiced romance routes, and a collectible relic system. Will you uncover the secrets of your lineage, or be consumed by the darkness? The choice is yours.

♦ Dual Morality System: Your choices shift your alignment between Humanity and Blood, affecting the story and endings.
♦ 4 Romance Routes: Pursue romance with a Vampire Lord, Hunter, Scholar, or Nightblade.
♦ Immersive Story: 3-4 hours of gameplay with partial voice acting and stunning artwork.
